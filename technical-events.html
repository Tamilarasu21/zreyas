Papyrus Pitch

  • Maximum number of participants in a team is THREE.
  • Papers need to be sent in the specified & suggested areas only.
  • Please mail your abstract to the respective mail-id.
  • The mail id should contain the following:
    1. Title of the Paper
    2. Name of the Participants
  • Abstract should not exceed 1 page and paper should not exceed 10 pages.
  • The teams will get 10 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
  • After submission of the soft copy, a panel of judges will go through the papers and if you are shortlisted to give the presentation, you will be notified.
  • The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
  • There should be minimum of 10 slides and it should not exceed 20.
  • TOPICS:
    1. Gender Equality
    2. Importance of Cyber Security
    3. Bio Energy Resources
    4. Disaster Management
    5. Basics of IC Engine
    6. Embedded Systems
