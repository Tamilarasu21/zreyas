ZREYAS'21

Beyond the barriers

With the objective to foster Gender Equality and equal opportunity to all, ZREYAS'21 is aforethought. ZREYAS'21 is an national level technical symposium conducted at the month of February by the Gender Equality Club of Kongu Engineering College. ZREYAS'21 will be an virtual fete for all those Technical and non - technical quester. Yes! You are right. Fete filled with technical, non - technical, culturals, fun events and what not. So all, clear your plans and get ready to enjoy and welcome ZREYAS'21!

General Rules

  • Every participant is expected to behave politely with organisers, volunteers and other participants.
  • Misconduct or potential malpractice in any form will be penalised and may include disqualification and/or ban.
  • Participants are advised to be present at the respective meeting platforms in advance to avoid last-minute hassles. Any request for a delay will not be entertained.
  • It is the participant's responsibility to have a stable internet connection. Dealing with any discontinuity in the event on the participant's side is not the organisers responsibility.
  • Participants must further abide by the specific rules of the individual competitions/events which are stated in the rule book

Papyrus Pitch

EVENT RULES
  • Maximum number of participants in a team is THREE.
  • Papers need to be sent in the specified & suggested areas only.
  • Please mail your abstract to the respective mail-id.
  • The mail id should contain the following:
    1. Title of the Paper
    2. Name of the Participants
  • Abstract should not exceed 1 page and paper should not exceed 10 pages.
  • The teams will get 10 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
  • The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
  • There should be minimum of 10 slides and it should not exceed 20.
  • TOPICS:
    1. Gender Equality
    2. Recent trends in Engineering
    3. Bio Energy Resources
    4. Embedded Systems
    5. Agriculture
    6. Disaster Management

Innovato

EVENT RULES
  • Your presentation should be 15 minutes long with time (5 minutes) for questions afterward.
  • Maximum number of participants is Three. Each member of your group (if your project is a group project) must play a significant role in the presentation.
  • Please mail your abstract along with objective of the project not exceeding 1 page to the respective mail-id.
  • The mail id should contain the following:
    1. Title of the Paper along with a 1 line objective.
    2. Name of the Participants
    3. Email Id & Mobile Number of the Authors
    4. Year of study, department and College of the Authors.
  • Start with an outline and end with a conclusion. At the beginning of your presentation, let your panel members know what to expect; at the end, concisely review what you’ve done
  • The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.

Let The Music Speak

EVENT RULES

Round 1

  • 3 members per team.
  • Each team will be asked 4 questions.
  • Guess the tamil song from the translated english lyrics.
  • 15 seconds will be given.

Round 2

  • There will be both english and tamil lyrics of that tamil song .
  • In that team members have to find the missing words within the time limit .

Note:

  • The teams selected in round 1 will go to round 2.
  • Juries decisions will be final,teams not following this will be disqualified.

Word Junction

EVENT RULES

Round 1 - WORD BUILDING

  • Individual participation.
  • 5 seconds time for each one.
  • The participants wants to tell the word related to the given word.
  • Example: Dark bright sun yellow.

Round 2 - ENGTAM or TAMENG

  • Individual participants
  • 5 seconds time for each one
  • Either English word or tamil word is given, the participants must continue the chain with tamil and English words alternatively.
  • Example: Bird kili Green maram leaves etc.

Do You Know Me

EVENT RULES

Round 1 - FIND ME

  • 2 members per team.
  • Each team will have 2 bgm's of any ad.
  • Guess the bgm within 10 secs.

Round 2 - FIND OUT

  • Each team will have 2 ads randomly.
  • Observe the ad given to you.
  • For each team, we will ask 3 questions from the ad.
  • Answer them within 15 secs.

Note:

  • The teams selected in round 1 will go to round 2.
  • Juries decisions will be final,teams not following this will be disqualified.
