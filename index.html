Gender Equality Club

"Strive for equality". With this motto in mind, we aim to foster equality in gender inorder to attain a socially responsible and sustainable environment especially among students. Kongu Engineering College

Kongu Engineering College is an internationally recognised leader in professional and career - oriented education. Kongu Engineering College is affiliated to Anna University Chennai. Kongu Engineering College has completed more than three decades of dedicated service to people of India and abroad in the field of Technical education.
